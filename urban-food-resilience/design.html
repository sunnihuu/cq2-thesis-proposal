Urban Agriculture Integration

Strategic placement of community gardens, rooftop farms, and vertical growing systems in underserved neighborhoods to increase local food production and create economic opportunities. Design interventions adapt to existing urban conditions, regulatory constraints, and community needs.

Distributed Food Networks

Development of distributed food processing and distribution nodes connecting urban producers, existing markets, and consumers while reducing transportation emissions. Prototype nodes demonstrate feasibility at neighborhood scale with potential for citywide expansion.

Infrastructure for Change

  • Adaptive reuse of underutilized urban land for food production
  • Cold storage and processing facilities in identified food deserts
  • Micro-mobility solutions for last-mile food distribution
  • Community education and skill-building programs
  • Policy frameworks and implementation roadmaps
