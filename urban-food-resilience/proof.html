Feasibility Scalability Roadmap

Proof of Feasibility

Design interventions are tested against economic, spatial, and regulatory constraints. Prototypes demonstrate viability in diverse neighborhood contexts, considering land availability, zoning, infrastructure capacity, and community needs.

Scalability & Expansion

Interventions designed for neighborhood-scale implementation with potential for citywide expansion. Economic models demonstrate cost-effectiveness and long-term sustainability. Community participation and local ownership ensure resilience and equity.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Pilot programs in 3-5 neighborhoods (Years 1-2)
  • Phase 2: Expansion to 15-20 neighborhoods (Years 2-5)
  • Phase 3: Citywide integration and system maturation (Years 5+)
  • Policy advocacy and regulatory reform throughout
  • Community capacity building and stakeholder engagement
